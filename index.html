About Me

Background

I am an experienced engineer who holds a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Virginia Tech and a Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering from Penn State. At the moment, I am enrolled in Post-Masters studies in Electrical & Computer Engineering at Johns Hopkins and am looking for faculty members interested in advising me as a working professional and potential doctoral student.

My research interests include signals & systems analysis, engineering acoustics, and analyzing sensor & system performance by applying data science techniques to engineering data. In terms of my research style, my strength is in empirical research based on real-world data. However, I welcome opportunities to grow by collaborating with individuals who may be stronger in theory or entrepreneurs who may gravitate to developing application-oriented solutions to solving technical and non-technical challenges. My curriculum vitae (CV) and contact information is available at the bottom of this website.

Beyond my academic and research interests, I have been an active member of the American Society of Mechanical Engineers since 2012. I also enjoy volunteering at a variety of different organizations in Baltimore City and the Washington DC Metropolitan Area in my spare time. My personal interests include reading about business / economics / psychology, taking massive open online courses unrelated to engineering, and I have produced music as an independent artist since 2008.
